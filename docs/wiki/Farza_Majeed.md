# Farza Majeed

**Farza Majeed** is a developer and founder of buildspace. In April 2026 he published [Farzapedia](Farzapedia.md)—a personal wiki compiled by an LLM from roughly 2,500 diary entries, Apple Notes, and iMessage exports into about 400 interlinked markdown articles.

His [announcement thread on X](https://x.com/FarzaTV/status/2040563939797504467) describes the project as agent-first memory: [Claude Code](https://docs.anthropic.com/en/docs/claude-code) navigates the wiki from `index.md` rather than relying on opaque [Retrieval_Augmented_Generation](Retrieval_Augmented_Generation.md) alone. [Andrej Karpathy](Andrej_Karpathy.md) highlighted Farzapedia as a concrete example of the [LLM Wiki](LLM_Wiki.md) idea.
