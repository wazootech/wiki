# Andrej Karpathy

**Andrej Karpathy** is a prominent AI researcher and developer. He is well known for his work at OpenAI and Tesla, as well as his popular tutorials on deep learning.

In early 2026, he popularized the [[llm-wiki]] design pattern for [[personal-knowledge]], summarizing it with the analogy:

> "Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase."

For detailed coverage of this methodology, refer to [[llm-wiki]].
