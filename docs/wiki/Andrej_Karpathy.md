# Andrej Karpathy

**Andrej Karpathy** is an AI researcher and educator, known for work at [OpenAI](https://openai.com) and [Tesla](https://www.tesla.com), tutorials on deep learning, and coining "vibe coding."

In early April 2026 he popularized the [LLM_Wiki](LLM_Wiki.md) pattern for [Personal_Knowledge](Personal_Knowledge.md):

- [LLM Knowledge Bases](https://x.com/karpathy/status/2039805659525644595) (3 April 2026)
- [LLM Wiki idea file](https://x.com/karpathy/status/2040470801506541998) and [gist](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f) (4 April 2026)

He summarized it with the analogy (see [Obsidian_Integration](Obsidian_Integration.md) as the typical viewer):

> "Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase."

He later highlighted [Farzapedia](Farzapedia.md) by [Farza Majeed](Farza_Majeed.md) as a concrete file-first personal wiki for agents.
